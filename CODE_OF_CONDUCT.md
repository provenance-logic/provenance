# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

The Provenance project is committed to respectful, professional collaboration. We expect all contributors, maintainers, and participants to:

- Be welcoming and inclusive to people of all backgrounds and experience levels
- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully
- Be considerate of differing viewpoints and approaches
- Focus on what is best for the project and its community

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following will not be tolerated:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Hostile, dismissive, or deliberately unconstructive communication
- Personal attacks or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers by opening a private issue or contacting the team directly. All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for enforcing this code of conduct. Participants who violate these standards may be warned, temporarily banned, or permanently removed from the project at the maintainers' discretion.
